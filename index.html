About Us

Welcome to Remine India, a leading startup in the field of e-waste and Li battery recycling. We are committed to creating a sustainable future by reducing electronic waste and minimizing its impact on the environment. Our innovative and eco-friendly recycling solutions help us recover valuable materials from discarded electronics and batteries, thereby reducing the need for mining new materials and conserving natural resources.
